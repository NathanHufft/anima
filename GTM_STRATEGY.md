# Anima Companion — Go-to-Market Strategy (2026)

**Product:** Anima — a frameless, transparent, always-on-top 3D anime desktop companion that lives on the user’s desktop, renders any .vrm avatar, thinks with any LLM (Claude, GPT, Grok, Ollama, Azure), speaks with offline or premium voices, and can act on the user’s computer via opt-in agent tools.

**Positioning:** The delightful, always-present AI friend who feels alive on your desktop — expressive, private-first, and useful without stealing your screen.

---

## 1. Vision & Value Proposition

**Vision:** Make personal AI feel like a living companion rather than a tab or app.

**Core Value Prop (one line):**  
“Anima is the expressive 3D anime companion who lives on your desktop, remembers you, speaks naturally, and helps you — privately and with your permission.”

**Differentiation pillars**
- **Presence:** Always-visible, click-through “ghost mode,” floating window — not a sidebar or tab.
- **Expression:** Real-time VRM avatar with mood-driven expressions, gestures, lip-sync, spring-bone physics.
- **Flexibility:** Any LLM (local or cloud), any voice (offline or premium), any .vrm body.
- **Agency with consent:** Opt-in tools that act on your computer inside a sandboxed workspace; every write/open/command requires explicit approval.
- **Privacy-first:** Keys encrypted at rest; local Ollama path; no telemetry by default.

## 2. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) & Personas

**Primary ICP**

**Secondary ICP**
- VTuber / anime fans who want a desktop pet that can also think and act.

- **Jordan (Engineer, 34):** Wants a local-first agent that can run commands in a sandbox and show expressions while pair-programming verbally.

## 4. Competitive Landscape & Positioning

**Direct alternatives**
- Static desktop pets / widgets (low intelligence).
- ChatGPT desktop / Raycast / Arc Max (powerful but hidden or tab-based).
- VRoid / Warudo / VSeeFace (great avatars, no brain or tools).

**Positioning map**
- X-axis: Visibility/Presence (hidden → always visible)
- Y-axis: Capability (chat only → agentic + expressive)

Anima wins the top-right quadrant: high presence + high capability with consent.

**Competitive narrative:** “Most AI hides in a window. Most pets have no brain. Anima is both — expressive, agentic, and yours.”

## 12. Risk Register & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| VRM loading or three-vrm CDN failure | Medium | High | Vendor three.js + three-vrm; offline fallback avatar; clear error + “how to fix” modal. |
| User enables shell tool and runs destructive command | Low | High | Default off; double confirmation; workspace sandbox; clear “this can delete files” warning. |
| Privacy backlash (keys, telemetry) | Medium | High | Default no telemetry; local-only mode prominent; publish privacy policy + data flow diagram. |
| LLM cost spikes (user abuse) | Medium | Medium | Rate limits per user; show cost estimate in settings; Pro tier absorbs overages. |
| Platform policy (Mac notarization, Windows SmartScreen) | High | Medium | Sign builds; maintain allow-list; clear “how to run” instructions. |
| Creator DMCA on shared VRMs | Low | Medium | Marketplace terms require original or licensed assets; takedown process. |
| Churn after novelty wears off | High | High | Weekly delight drops; memory & habit loops; community events. |
